About us

Dublin Events Guide is an info hub listing kid friendly restaurants, fun activities and upcoming events, as well as featuring unique Irish brands and retailers.

It's a comprehensive guide to Dublin for those who want to explore and enjoy the capital and is fast becoming the go to guide for everything to do with your baby, child and teen.

Dublinguide.ie has been designed by me for tourists, parents, grandparents, childminders and for anyone who wakes up and wonders what to do with the kids today.

Hopefully this site will become your go to guide for everything to do with your baby, toddler and child in Dublin. Enjoy, I hope you have as much fun exploring Dublin with kids as I do.
