About

Dow are B.W. Smith, J.G.Wold and A. Roberts. We at DOW are passionate about cars and models. And when we say passionate… we really mean it. B.W. Smith, a true petrolhead, is the driving force behind Dow. From proud owner of die cast Ford Mustang Boss 429, to driving the real life breathing Dodge Challenger in those wild student years, to an owner os few timeless beauties seen also on this site. The pursue for dream wheels, from collecting matchbox cars, to driving in historic rallies after college, the passion has become his way of life, hobby and a job.

Sharing our love for anything motor related brought us together. Our different areas of expertise made us realise that a business venture together would set us apart from what was already out there. We don’t just want to sell you a model, we want you to love what you purchase! When you purchase a model from DOW you know that you are dealing with people who share your passion and take a pride in providing impeccable customer service.

In 2012 the Dow’s Model Car Workshop opened its virtual doors to our customers from all around the globe. We bring you customized model cars of highest quality, with deep knowledge in the field. You can expect us to manage you the best deals and the most sought out items in the market.

We specialize in 1/43 and 1/18 scale model cars by manufacturers including C.A.R.S, Modelthat, Nr.one, Aston Originals, Precious and many others. As enthusiasts, our interests are mainly in European classics, but surely, we do not discriminate. We regularly bring interesting additions to our collection from all corners of the car world. What matters to us is the quality and the value for investment.

And if you do not find what you are looking for, stay tuned or throw us with a message. There are many well-loved models in the pipeline which we will add to our catalog as they are released. Meanwhile, get to know our highlights contenders for this month. And don't forget our blog section for stories, intrigue and tidbits from our contributors A. Roberts, J.G. Wolf and guests. Or apply to become a contributor yourself.
